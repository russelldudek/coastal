Competitive landscape

The AI consulting battlefield is moving closer to deployment.

That creates pressure—but it also makes Coastal's combination of agility, trust, Salesforce depth, AI Operations thinking, and TCS-backed scale more valuable.

Model providers

OpenAI is moving closer to enterprise deployment.

Deep alliances with global consulting firms and increased deployment focus mean model providers are becoming more influential in services ecosystems—not just software vendors.

Anthropic ecosystem

Partner networks are becoming strategic channels.

Certification, responsible use, technical credibility, and repeatable deployment capability are becoming meaningful differentiators for enterprise partners.

Global consultancies

The giants are pairing strategy with forward-deployed engineering.

Large firms are building embedded AI delivery models and industry solutions. Their scale is real; so are their cost, complexity, and speed disadvantages.

Coastal opportunity

Enterprise capability. Midmarket agility. Human trust.

The opportunity is not to imitate a global consultancy. It is to combine the best of enterprise delivery discipline with the directness and speed clients already value in Coastal.
